REACHING HAND

About us

Reaching Hand is a registered Charitable Trust started in 1996 to work with vulnerable children, marginalized women and deprived families empowering them to lead a life of hope, self-worth and dignity. It envisions a promising future for all by acting as a catalyst for social change. Empowerment of children, women and families from weaker sections of society through quality education, health care and skill development is the key focus of Reaching Hand
